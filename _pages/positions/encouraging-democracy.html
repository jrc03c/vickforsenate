Encouraging Democracy

  • I support same day voter registration.
  • I will work to repeal the highly restrictive Texas Photo Voter ID Law.
  • I will introduce legislation to allow online voter registration.
  • It is essential that we create a nonpartisan redistricting commission to end unfair gerrymandering.
  • I support the full disclosure of all funding sources for political advertisements on television, radio, in print, mailers, handouts and online.
  • I will support legislation to strengthen the Texas Ethics Commission to insure officeholders adhere to the highest ethical standards.
